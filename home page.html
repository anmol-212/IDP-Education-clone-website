Register For IELTS

How to Register for IELTS exam?

There are there ways in which you can register for your IELTS.

◾ Register online and pay the test fee using a credit card
◾ Register in person at any IDP branch or Input Node
◾ Register by courier and pay the test fee by Demand Draft or ICICI deposit/HDFC deposit slip

Option 1: Register online and pay the test fee

Log on to www.ieltsidpindia.com
◾ Select the option "Register for IELTS"
◾ Select your test date and test city (you will see the seat availability status)
◾ Complete the online application form
◾ Pay the test fee using a valid credit card (Master / Visa)

Once your registration is complete, you will see a confirmation on your computer screen (you may print and keep this with you for future reference). A copy of the confirmation will also be sent in hard copy or on your registered email address

You’ll need to courier a photocopy of your Passport (first and last page), and any observation pages to the IDP Head Office (address provided below). Failing which you will NOT be allowed to sit the test. Candidates need to carry original passport on test day.

Option 2: Register in person at any IDP branch or Input Nod

Walk into any IDP branch/head offices or your nearest Input Node. You can register using either of the following methods to register:

Method - I
◾ Register online using the computer at the office and pay the test fee. You’ll get an immediate confirmation of your seat (this is identical to online registration)
◾ Submit a photocopy of your Passport (first and last page), and observation pages, if any
◾ Submit the duly signed Terms & Conditions document. Do read it properly before handing over
◾ Receive your instant acknowledgement letter that confirms your seat in the test

Method - II
◾ Fill the application form available at the IDP office/Input Node
◾ Sign the Terms & Conditions document provided with the application form
◾ Append a photocopy of your Passport (first and last page), and observation pages, if any
◾ Pay the test fee either by
◾ A valid credit / debit / ATM card, or
◾ Demand Draft (drawn in favour of "IDP Education India Private Limited, payable at New Delhi"). It should be made at any bank listed in the approved list of banks (provided in the Terms & Conditions document)
◾ Depositing cash in an ICICI bank branch via a customised ICICI deposit slip, which can be either downloaded from the IDP IELTS website or collected from any IDP branch office/ Input Node. The original customer copy of the customised ICICI deposit slip needs to be handed over to IDP branch office or the Input Node as proof of payment

About Us

IDP: IELTS Australia

IELTS Australia Pty Ltd is a wholly owned subsidiary of IDP Education Ltd, one of the world's leading international education organisations offering student placement in Australia, the USA, Canada, New Zealand and the UK. IDP Education has more than 45 years' experience in international higher education.

IELTS Australia Pty Ltd manages a network of more than 100 IELTS test centres in over 45 countries . IELTS Australia Pty Ltd ACN 008 664 766.

In 1989, a partnership between the University of Cambridge Local Examinations Syndicate, the British Council and IDP was formed to develop and manage IELTS (International English Language Testing System). Since then the network of test centres managed by IELTS Australia Pty Ltd has grown to more than a 100 test centres across the world.

The quality and reliability of IELTS has been demonstrated through its recognition as the world's premier test for English proficiency. Please contact ielts.india@idp.com for further information.
